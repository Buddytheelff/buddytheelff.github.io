Urbanization

Urbanization is the process of making an area more urban. Built up area or BUA is one aspect used to measure this process.

The BUA of metropolitan statistical areas and micropolitan statistical areas is measured by taking the area covered by structures divided by the total available land.

In this example, the BUA for every census year since 1900 is displayed. Just select an area from the map and view the chart on the left.

The scale in which this data was processed was at a 250 meter resolution.
